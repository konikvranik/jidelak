Congratulations!

You have installed Jidelak application, which is ready to import templates of restaurants.

Jidelak is designed as a viewer for the daily menu. The menu must be fetched from the website of the restaurant. In order the Jidelak knows where to load the menu and what each field on the website means there is needed a template.

Templates are in XSL format and you can create them either, or download templates which someone has already prepared. For these purposes there is a discussion forum, where you will find these templates and instructions how to create your own template.

Forum is on this address: https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/jidelak.

Thank you for choosing Jidelak I wish you a good taste.

PS: Anything you will miss in Jidelak, or if you hit the problem that can be addressed, please let me know via Issue tracking at https://code.google.com/p/jidelak/issues/list.
